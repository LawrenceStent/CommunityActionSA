Personal Freedom

Encourage open dialogue where every member can express their views freely. Avoid imposing rigid structures or rules that limit personal autonomy.

Equality

Establish inclusive practices that respect and incorporate diverse perspectives, especially those marginalized in broader society. Ensure equal access to resources and decision-making processes for all members.

Mutual Aid

Create systems for members to support each other, such as sharing resources, skills, and knowledge. Organize initiatives like community kitchens, cooperative childcare, or skill-sharing workshops.

Direct Democracy

Hold regular, open meetings where all members can participate in decision-making. Use consensus-based decision-making processes to ensure all voices are heard and respected.

Decentralization

Form smaller, autonomous working groups within the larger group to tackle specific tasks or projects. Allow these sub-groups the freedom to operate independently while maintaining communication with the main group.

Reduction of Coercion and Violence

Promote non-violent conflict resolution methods within the group. Establish a culture of mutual respect and understanding, aiming to resolve disagreements through dialogue.

Environmental Sustainability

Implement environmentally friendly practices in all group activities, such as recycling, reducing waste, and promoting sustainable agriculture. Organize community clean-ups or campaigns to protect local natural resources.

Creativity and Innovation

Create an open environment where members feel free to propose new ideas and initiatives. Encourage artistic and cultural expressions within the community.

Community Responsibility

Empower members to take ownership of local issues and actively work towards solutions. Promote education and awareness campaigns to inform the community about their rights and responsibilities.

Resilience and Flexibility

Develop contingency plans and diverse strategies to address potential challenges. Encourage adaptability and quick responses to changing circumstances or emergencies.
